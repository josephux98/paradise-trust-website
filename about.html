About

Paradise Family & Social Welfare Trust is dedicated to serving the elderly, the poor, and vulnerable communities with compassion and dignity. Since 2015, we have worked to provide shelter, healthcare support, education, and community programs that uplift lives and restore hope. Our mission is to build a caring society where every individual is valued, supported, and empowered to live with dignity.

Committed to Stewardship, Service & Community Impact

Supporting Families, Preserving Legacy, Serving Communities

Paradise Family Trust is dedicated to responsible stewardship, long-term community support, and meaningful charitable impact. Through careful management of resources and thoughtful partnerships, we aim to create opportunities that strengthen families and uplift communities.

Our trust focuses on sustainable giving, educational support, and initiatives that promote stability and growth for future generations. By working with community partners and trusted organizations, we ensure that our contributions make a lasting and measurable difference.

Our Journey of Stewardship

Since its founding, Paradise Family Trust has been guided by a commitment to responsible stewardship, community support, and long-term impact. Our journey reflects years of careful planning, meaningful partnerships, and dedication to strengthening families and communities.

2015

Trust Established

Paradise Family & Social Welfare Trust was officially founded on 21st March 2015 by Mr. Julies M. Lazar, with the aim of serving society through charitable initiatives focused on elderly care, education, and community welfare.

2016

Community Welfare Initiatives

The trust began planning and organizing charitable activities aimed at supporting elderly citizens and economically disadvantaged families within the community.

2018

Expansion of Social Support Programs

The trust strengthened its mission by promoting educational initiatives, community development activities, and assistance for people in need.

2021

Vision for Elderly Care

The trust expanded its vision by planning to establish an old-age care initiative under the banner “Snehakoodaram”, aiming to provide compassionate care and support for elderly individuals irrespective of caste, religion, or background.

2024

Growing Community Impact

The trust continues to expand its social welfare programs including educational support, medical assistance, and charitable activities benefiting the broader community.
